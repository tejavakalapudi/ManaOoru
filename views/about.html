Chandravaram is a village panchayat located in the West Godavari district of Andhra-Pradesh state,India. The latitude 16.955378 and longitude 81.702018 are the geocoordinate of the Chandravaram. Amaravathi is the state capital for Chandravaram village. It is located around 176 kilometer away from Chandravaram..
